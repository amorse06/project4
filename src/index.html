What is 3-1-1?

Basically, 311 is a type of citizens’ hotline. It’s a simple way for you to report problems or ask questions about your community without tying up emergency lines or going through frustrating municipal channels. The 311 wesite can be used to forward complaints to local government, local utilities, and other public services to get the problem fixed or answer your question.
